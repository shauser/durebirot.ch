For the last few days of our trip we're on a ship in the Ha Long Bay. Since we don't know whether we have any internet connectivity at all, these posts are being posted automatically. Please note that we might be unable to answer the mailbox during this time.

Maybe some of you already noticed it: Starting from the 14th of April our posts' titles became different than before. We decided to give our posts titles of different movies - sometimes slightly modified.

The idea of this blog was to share our impressions with family and friends at home. Now we go one step further and give you the chance to taste Vietnam. And this is how the competition works:

The person who figures out the most of the original movie titles we used in our blog posts' titles wins a price. To participate send the list to [contact@durebirot.ch](mailto:contact@durebirot.ch). Back home we'll check the emails and inform the winners. If multiple people have the same number of correct entries, the first one to send them in wins.

The first price is a meal with different authentic Vietnamese dishes we've eaten here and we'll cook for you (and your partner). The second price is a set composed of two drip filters and a pound of Vietnamese coffee. Of course we're also going to demonstrate how it works if you're interested.

We're looking forward to cook for you.
